About us


Keshab +3 Science College, a dream project of “Gyatri Educational & Charitable Trust”, was incepted in 2016 under the noble vision and guidance of Honorable Mr.Susant K Rout – the esteemed chairman of "Keshab". since inception the college is running smoothly keeping the students future in mind, driving each and every students to their dream carrier. It’s been a glorious journey towards the perfection and the campus cracker students are the only witness of our glorious achievements. We believe in the harmonious relationship between college , students and recruiters .




Brand Legacy since 2011

The college is functioning in dual modes.


Full Time Course

Providing the +3 science (Bsc.CS (H), Bsc.ITM (H), Bsc.MATH (H) and BCA) education under the affiliation of UTKAL University and is recognized by Govt. of Odisha.
Part Time Course

Providing training in IT education and Preplacement Training a stand-alone brand name “LIT Susant K Rout Center of Excellence” in producing the “best brains” of the state.
